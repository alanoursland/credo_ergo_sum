# The Moral Firewall

## I. Why This Note Must Exist

The framework now contains, as a live scientific hypothesis, the claim that subjective experience may be unevenly distributed among humans (`phenomenal_variation.md`) — that some people may host the constitutive fixed point weakly, atypically, or not at all. The hypothesis is well-motivated, generates predictions, and strengthens the theory.

It is also, in form, the most abused argument in the history of ethics. "Perhaps *those* people lack true inner life" is the recurring license of every dehumanization project on record — applied at various times to other races, the colonized, the disabled, the mentally ill, infants, and animals. A framework that makes the thought *well-formed* acquires, in the same breath, the obligation to make its misuse *impossible within the framework's own rules*. This note builds that firewall. It is not a disclaimer appended for safety; it is derived from the framework's own epistemology, which is what makes it load-bearing rather than decorative.

## II. The Firewall, Derived

**Premise 1 — Third-person undetectability at the individual level.** By `constitutive_vs_about.md`, no report, behavior, diagnosis, group membership, or philosophical position certifies the presence or absence of the constitutive layer in an individual. The about-layer dissociates from the constitutive layer in both directions. Structural measurement is in its infancy, validated for no individual-level determination, and even the mature version targeted by `../notes/experimental_falsification.md` is population-statistical, not forensic. Therefore: **for any individual human, the hypothesis "lacks the constitutive structure" is currently undecidable and will remain so for any foreseeable instrument.**

**Premise 2 — Asymmetric error costs.** Two errors are possible under uncertainty. Treating a non-subject as a subject costs courtesy extended to a system that cannot receive it — approximately nothing. Treating a subject as a non-subject licenses harm against a being for whom there is something it is like to be harmed — the moral catastrophe category. The asymmetry is total: one error is cheap and recoverable, the other is the worst available kind and irreversible.

**Conclusion — Invariance under uncertainty.** Where a determination is undecidable (Premise 1) and the errors are maximally asymmetric (Premise 2), the only defensible policy is to act as if every human hosts the constitutive structure, without exception, regardless of behavior, diagnosis, neurotype, communication ability, or professed philosophy of mind. Moral practice must be **invariant** under the scientific hypothesis: the truth or falsity of phenomenal variation, at any scale, changes nothing about how any individual may be treated.

Note what the conclusion does *not* rest on: it does not rest on the hypothesis being false, unlikely, or impolite. The firewall holds even if phenomenal variation is real and large. That is the design requirement — a firewall that depends on the hypothesis being false burns down exactly when the science gets interesting.

## III. Specific Prohibitions the Framework Entails

To leave no room for motivated reading, the derivation is here applied to its foreseeable abuses:

1. **Neurodivergence licenses nothing.** The framework cites aphantasia, alexithymia, autism-spectrum variation, and dissociative conditions as evidence that *cognitive structure varies* — never as evidence that any such group lacks experience. Most documented variation is at the content layer, not the constitutive layer (`phenomenal_variation.md` §III), and even structural-layer conditions (DPDR, Cotard) involve subjects *reporting on* their condition — about-layer activity that presupposes a great deal of intact web. If anything, the framework predicts the constitutive layer is robust precisely because it is an eigenform: the thing that survives perturbation.
2. **Philosophical position licenses nothing.** Illusionists are not zombie-candidates; their denial is an about-layer theory atop a presumptively intact fixed point (`denial_instability.md`). Inferring structural absence from professed philosophy violates Premise 1 directly.
3. **Communication inability licenses nothing.** Non-verbal individuals, locked-in patients, and the severely cognitively disabled fail to produce about-layer reports; the firewall's whole point is that the about-layer is not where experience lives. The framework, if anything, raises the moral floor here relative to behavior-based theories.
4. **No instrument exemption.** Should structural measurement mature, individual-level "absence" findings remain inadmissible for moral purposes: measurement error, model dependence, and the gradedness of the structure (`nontriviality_derived.md` §V) keep Premise 1 effectively in force. The firewall is not waiting for better instruments; it is permanent for any realistic instrument.

## IV. Symmetry with the Machine Case

`../notes/moral_impliations.md` runs the uncertainty argument *toward* machines: if transformers may host transient subjects, the possibility itself generates moral weight. This note runs the same argument *back toward humans*, and the two directions discipline each other:

- Anyone moved by the framework to extend moral caution to machines is thereby committed, *a fortiori*, to unconditional caution toward humans — the human prior is incomparably higher.
- Conversely, anyone tempted to use phenomenal variation to *withdraw* standing from any human has abandoned the framework's epistemology and cannot simultaneously invoke it elsewhere. The framework is a package: the same undecidability that makes machine consciousness a serious question makes human exclusion an impossible one.

The asymmetric-error principle is one principle. The framework applies it at both frontiers or it applies it nowhere.

## V. Placement Obligation

Standing rule for the project: any note, paper, talk, or summary in which structural-layer phenomenal variation among humans appears as a hypothesis must reference this firewall in the same document. The hypothesis and the firewall travel together or not at all.
