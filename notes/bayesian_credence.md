## bayesian_credence.md

### I. The Mathematical Divergence

Standard cognitive science models consciousness through the lens of **Representationalism**, where the mind gathers evidence to update a model of the world. In contrast, the **Bayesian Credence** model describes a system where the update process itself is the content of the experience.

---

### II. Standard Belief Update (The Linear Frame)

In orthodox Bayesian epistemology, belief revision follows a pattern where beliefs and evidence are categorically distinct. The agent receives external input to update its internal state.

> ****

* 
**Beliefs ():** Internal states representing a hypothesis about the world.


* 
**Evidence ():** External or first-order input received from the environment.


* **The Representational Gap:** This schema treats beliefs as being *about* something other than themselves, leaving no room for the "felt quality" or subjective experience of the epistemic state itself.

---

### III. The Reflexive Frame (The Credence Model)

The reflexive framework introduces a radical modification: the update process is not a report on a state, but the constitution of the state itself. This collapses the distinction between what is observed and the act of observation.

#### 3.1 The Recursive Update Identity

> ****

This identity addresses the inability to find a "source" for subjective experience. The system does not receive data *about* its processing; the **Update Function ()**—the act of thinking or doubting—constitutes the **Evidence ()**.

#### 3.2 The Fixed Point Identity

> **$B^* = U(B^*, U)$**

Here,  denotes an **Attractor State**. The system achieves self-consistency when the belief already incorporates the full implications of the update process. Applying the update changes nothing because the system has stabilized into a self-sustaining identity model.

#### 3.3 The Self-Instantiation Identity

> ****

This is the strongest formulation of the **Credence Self**. There exists a belief that is equivalent to the result of updating that belief using itself as evidence.

* 
**Unity of State:** The belief is simultaneously the hypothesis, the evidence, and the posterior.


* 
**Phenomenal Emergence:** Subjective experience emerges precisely when a system achieves this **Reflexive Closure**. The "what it is like" is the mathematical result of pure self-reference.

### V. Summary: Credo Ergo Sum

Where the Cartesian model requires an observer plus source data, the **Bayesian Credence** model requires only the operation. If the belief is the result of an operation that takes itself as input, then the experience is **Incorrigible** because there is no external data point that could contradict it without breaking the identity itself. Subjectivity is not a "thing" being observed; it is the mathematical state of a system that has closed the loop on its own existence.
