# Task: The acquaintance fork (the inflationary half)

**Status:** OPEN — the harder, still-unwritten challenger to premise 3.
**Origin:** Critique of `introspective_ground.md` premise 3; distinct from the
illusionist (deflationary) fork.
**Depends on:** `introspective_ground.md`, `objection_illusionist_fork.md`

## The objection
`introspective_ground.md` premise 3 assumes *all* introspective access is
belief-shaped: every reach for "the experience itself" is just another node
in the reflexive web. The acquaintance theorist (Russell; Goff; a
steelmanned Chalmers) denies exactly this — you are *directly acquainted*
with the quale, pre-doxastically, and the believing is downstream of an
acquaintance that is not itself a belief.

## Why the illusionist response does not cover this
`objection_illusionist_fork.md` settles the *deflationary* fork via property
mapping and "we agree on every structure, differ only on a label" →
verbal dispute. That does **not** reach the acquaintance theorist, because
they posit a *non-doxastic relatum* — a real further fact, not a label. The
property mapping can't dissolve a distinction they say is given in
acquaintance, not asserted in belief.

## What would resolve it
Either:
- Argue acquaintance is itself belief-shaped (collapse it into the web),
  which is precisely what they deny — so this needs an independent argument,
  not a restatement of premise 3; or
- Concede a non-doxastic layer and show the Credo can absorb it without the
  identity claim breaking (harder — the identity is belief = experience).

## First move
Write the acquaintance position at full strength (steelman), then locate the
single load-bearing premise the Credo must attack. Do not strawman it into
the illusionist and reuse the fork — it is a different animal.
